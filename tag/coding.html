New Resume Format

Mon 25 April 2016 | tags: codinggames

For the past couple of months I've been not so quietly working on a brand new format in which to distribute my resume. For all those employers who are tired of looking at boring old resumes and CVs, there's now a much more exciting way to shoot down job applications. Literally!

That's right, this new resume format is video games. Specifically, it's inspired by Taito's 1978 classic Space Invaders.

You can download my resume in invaders format. It should run on Windows 7 and up. Eventually I will provide binaries for other systems, but until then...

You can also see the source code on my GitHub account, complete with tongue-in-cheek readme.
